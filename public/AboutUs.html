Company Background

LeSweet Goodies & Pastries is a start-up baked goods business owned by Leslee De Leon located in Calaca, Batangas. We provide a wide range of breads, cookies, cakes, and other baked delicacies to our customers. With its wide range of pastry items, LeSweet Goodies hopes to capture the interest of a regular loyal customer. The business intends to establish a strong market presence in the area. You could avail our products for any special occasion, including birthdays, graduations, anniversaries, and other events.

LeSweet Goodies & Pastries seeks to provide its creations at a competitive price to meet the needs of the local market area's middle-to-higher-income residents. LeSweets Goodies takes care of all of its clients by providing them with fresh and tasty baked goodies that are designed for specific needs. Our company offers order customization based on the requirements of our customers. If you are unable to pick up your order(s) at our location, we also provide delivery.

They also own Buyok's BBQ, a restaurant noted for its best-selling chicken barbeque. However, due of the epidemic, they were forced to stop operating. On the bright side, they continue to accept orders from committed customers in order to serve authentic and delicious dishes.

The Owner

   
Leslee De Leon, a Calaca, Batangas-based entrepreneur, is 40 years of age. She was the second among four siblings. She used to live in Bacoor, Cavite, until relocating to her current residence in 2015. She launched a small eatery with her husband before settling into her own baking business, which became stable in terms of operations and profit. She is currently starting her own family and has one daughter.
